Don't be slacking

Get your work done. Now. Oh, you need Joe to make this or that thing? Just tell him and keep on working. It's Joe's problem now.

Keep track

"Is this thing done? What do we need next? What's the roadmap? Hey Charlie, do you have the link to the cloud folder with all the documents?"
No more. Make shared notes, update them and pin the important information on top. This way you have no excuse.

Interact

Human beings have to interact with each other. If something isn't clear, leave a comment as you'd do on your favourite social network.

One service

This is a webapp. It means it's a website, but it's optimized to be used as an actual application. No need to download anything, just go to the website on your favourite device or operating system.
Note: if your favourite operating system isn't Linux you should rethink your priorities.
